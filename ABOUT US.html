About Us

Who We Are

Hatif Motor Dealers was established in 2016 in Nairobi with a mission to deliver high-quality vehicles to our clients with honesty and professionalism. Starting as a small showroom, we’ve evolved into a trusted dealership offering new and used cars, servicing, and vehicle financing solutions.

Why Choose Us

  • Trusted by thousands of satisfied customers
  • Expertise and dependable customer service
  • Transparent and fair pricing
  • Reliable after-sales support

Our Philosophy

“Drive Your Dream, Own the Road” — More than a slogan, it’s a promise we make to each of our customers.

Leadership
