HATIF MOTOR DEALERS

Hatif Motor Dealers Shop was founded in 2016 in Nairobi with the vision of bringing quality vehicles to the local market. From a modest showroom to a full-fledged dealership, we’ve grown based on trust, transparency, and excellent service. We now offer new & used cars, servicing, and financing solutions.

Meet the Owner

Maya Hatif is the founder and driving force behind Hatif Motor Dealers. With a background in engineering and a passion for cars, Maya started this business to offer honest car sales and dependable support. She remains actively involved in ensuring clients are satisfied every step of the way.
